About Me

As a PhD candidate at UChicago, I am involved in quantum simulation research, a subset of quantum computing where we engineer interactions matter to emulate naturally occuring or contrived phenomena. The matter we use are atoms trapped in tweezers made of laser light, we engineer interactions using light, electric, and magnetic fields.
